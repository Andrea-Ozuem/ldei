About MBS Training

Introducing Masters of Business in the Street

The interweave solutions international USA in collaboration with LDEI Presents Masters of Business in the Street (MBS) Entrepreneurship Training to women, orphans And widows.

The Masters of Business in the Street (MBS) reaches people in the street that have low education and little time to study. MBS graduates have increased income and savings by 64% and improved their quality of life by 74%.

How our Program works

At MBS, the participants learn the 6Ps of business which are PLAN, PRODUCT, PROCESS, PRICE, PROMOTION and PAPERWORK. With the 6Ps of business, the participants can improve their businesses.

They also learn the three (3) plans of success which are: success in your Business, home and serving your community. Summing up all they learn, they are taught how to improve and balance their wheel of life circle and have better homes.

Past MBS PROGRAMS

MBS Training of Orphans and Widows

For two months, we trained 18 young orphans and 25 widows. Most of the widows were petty traders and farmers mostly form Dafara Village, a suburb of Kuje Area Council, Abuja.

Before the graduation on 26th May 2023, the participants carried out a community service projects at the DAFARA VILLAGE as a way for the participants to give back to the community. In conclusion, the young orphans and widows were able to gain a valuable knowledge and attain self-reliance which is true success in their business, home and community.
