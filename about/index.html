Who we are

About LDEI

It is said that when a woman attains a state of holistic balance - mental, financial, emotional, and physical well-being, that sense of balance spreads through her home and community, fostering an environment of peace and progress. We are a platform committed to the wellness of women on the go.

Mission

Our mission is to empower and uplift women from all backgrounds by providing them with the right tools, resources, education and support they need to achieve personal and professional growth.

Vision

We envision a world of empowered women where each lady has a balanced wheel of life resulting in a transformed society.

Aims and Objectives

  • Empowering Women: Providing women with the right tools, skills, and knowledge needed to achieve their personal and professional success.
  • Education and Skill Development: Offering educational programs, workshops, and training sessions to help women develop skills in areas like leadership, entrepreneurship, and financial literacy.
  • Promoting Gender Equality: Advocating for women's rights and promoting gender equality in various spheres of life, including in the workplace, politics, and society.
  • Support and Mentorship: Creating networks and mentorship programs to support women in their personal and professional growth.
  • Health and Well-being: Addressing issues related to women's health and well-being, including physical health, mental health, and safety.
  • Economic Empowerment: Helping women become financially independent through entrepreneurship, job placement, and career development initiatives.
  • Advocacy and Awareness: Raising awareness about issues affecting women and advocating for policy changes that support women's rights and empowerment.
