Giving Back to Our Community

At the Ladies Domain and Empowerment Initiative (LDEI), we believe that real change begins at the grassroots. Community service is at the heart of everything we do, as we strive to create meaningful impact in the lives of individuals and communities.

Through a variety of programs, we address critical needs, empower young girls and women, and promote sustainable change. Every event we host, every partnership we form, and every initiative we champion is driven by a commitment to uplift, empower, and inspire our community.

Past Community Service Events

Pad a Girl Child

In 2024, many girls in Nigeria’s Federal Capital Territory (FCT) still struggle to access basic hygiene needs. To address this, The Ladies Domain and Empowerment Initiative (LDEI) launched the Pad a Girl Child outreach in Old GIWA Community, FCT, Abuja, focused on menstrual and personal hygiene for girls aged 11-15.

LDEI distributed hygiene kits with essentials like sanitary pads, soap, undergarments and toothbrushes, making a real impact on their well-being. Initiatives like Pad a Girl Child exemplify LDEI’s commitment to empowering young girls by ensuring access to basic necessities.

Community Service

For two months, we trained 18 young orphans and 25 widows. So many of the widows were petty traders and farmers mostly form Dafara Village, a suburb of Kuje Area Council.

Before the graduation on 26th May 2023, the participants carried out a community service projects at the DAFARA VILLAGE as a way for the participants to give back to the community. In conclusion, the young orphans and widows were able to gain a valuable knowledge and attained self-reliance which is true success in their Business, Home and Community.
