What we do

WE ARE A PEOPLE COMMITTED TO THE CHANGE WE WANT TO SEE!

WELLNESS

What can one do with a faulty body? At LDEI, as part of our mission of ensuring every woman has a balanced wheel of life, we are committed to encouraging physical wellness activities, educational seminars and also humanitarian outreaches to this end. In lieu of this, we organise:

  • Pad a girl child program
  • Physical wellness seminars and trainings with certified coaches

WEALTH CREATION

We believe that every woman should be an empowered woman. We are committed to improving the financial state of women and hence curtailing the tragic state women find themselves in as a result of finances. We do not just want to empower with resources, we also want to teach the average woman how to maximise resources to create wealth.
We create a platform where women can share their trade and patronise themselves to ensure her productivity. In lieu of this, we organise:

  • heavily discounted offers and scholarships to women to undertake Master of Business in the street by interweave solutions, USA.
  • business dinner seminar where we invite professionals and business organisations that women can leverage on.

RECREATION

We believe that hard work should be balanced with recreation. We are interested in promoting an active and fun lifestyle through high-touch interactions and activities that support a better quality of life. We host:

  • Picnics.
  • Quarterly gist corners where women can voice their concerns and are ably assisted.
  • End of year gathering to mark, acknowledge and celebrate we women that strive daily for our families, societies whilst not compromising on our goals.
