Partners

At the Ladies Domain and Empowerment Initiative (LDEI), we are dedicated to empowering women and uplifting underserved communities across Nigeria. Our impact grows through the strength of partnerships with organizations and individuals who share our commitment to creating positive change.

By partnering with us, you join a network focused on advancing health, education, and economic empowerment for women. Together, we can open doors, provide resources, and foster resilience, creating a lasting impact for women and their communities.
